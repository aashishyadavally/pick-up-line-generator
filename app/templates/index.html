Project Overview
Project Description:









We created an AI that returns a pick-up line when the user inputs a prompt. When the bot returns a pick-up line, it will then proceed to judge the pick-up line as though it were the user's grandmother. The bot outputs a creative pick-up line for the user to use for their own purpose.

Why we chose to make a pickup line generator:


If you've ever been in a romantic dilemma, you know exactly how it feels to not have a pick-up line ready! It is imperative to make social interactions more comfortable and easygoing. Speaking to your love interest can be extremely nerve-racking and tense, so a pick-up line handcrafted by a certified grandmoâ€‹ther will certainly seal the deal!

Our Process

Week 1: 
During the 1st week, we learned the basics of python. We learned the different data types, variables, loops, if statements, and different built-in functions. Our main focus was on dictionaries as a way to prepare for the next stage of learning.

Week 2:
During the 2nd week of AI camp we picked a project topic and collected around 2,400 data and had about 1,500 pick up lines ready. We cleaned out our data and collected more. We learned the usage of Pandas and learned to use data frames and manipulate the data inside them.

Week 3: 
The last week of AI camp, we broke up into 2 teams (Frontend and backend teams) and worked on different parts of the project. The front end team worked on designing the website while the backend team worked on the applications and training the data.
